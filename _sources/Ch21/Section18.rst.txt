References

1. Stefik, M. Introduction to Knowledge Systems, Pages 608-621, 656-666.

Summary:

1. Configuration is a process that assigns values to variables to satisfy constraints.

2. The configuration process starts with specifications and its output is an arrangement model.

3. In the process,abstract and partial solutions are expanded and refined iteratively to arrive at the final arrangement model.
