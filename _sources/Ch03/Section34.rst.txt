This lesson cover the following topics:1. Knowledge representation and
Reasoning using that representation is the key to problem-solving.2.
Semantic Networks are one of the many ways for knowledge
representation.3. Pathways along spreading activation networks could
potentially help with memorizing and recalling solutions instead of
solving them every time for new recurring problems.
