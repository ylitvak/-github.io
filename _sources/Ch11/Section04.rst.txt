|Classifying Birds| 
So here are four of the animals that David classified as birds. Let us
look at the kind of features that he examined in order to classify
whether or not an animal was a bird. He may have used several features,
some of which he articulated, others that he may not have articulated.
So whether a animal has wings? Whether it has feathers? Whether it has a
beak? And so on and so forth. One could add more features here if one
wished to do so. We do classification all the time. AI agents need to do
classification all the time also. Why? Why is classification so
ubiquitous?
