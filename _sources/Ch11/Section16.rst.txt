|Order of Concepts| 
To summarize then, concepts can be of many different kinds. Fro very
formal, concepts, like Axiomatic concepts, to less formal concepts, like
Exemplar concepts. Of course, we can, if we want less formal concepts,
like exemplar concepts, philosophers often talk about concepts called
qualia, Q-U-A-L-I-A. Qualia for the raw sensations that we may get from
our sensors. And example of qualia is, bitterness. I’m sure you’ve come
across some bitter fruit, some time or the other. And you can even taste
it, inside your mouth right now, if you wanted to. But it’s very hard to
communicate what a qualia is to anyone else, what. Your notion of a
genesis to anyone else.
