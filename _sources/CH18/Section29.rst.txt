Section 29: Summary
::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

Analogical-reasoning is inherently transfer-based learning where we abstract over prior knowledge to discern transferable nuggets of knowledge and then transfer them onto a new problem in order to solve the new problem.
