Constraint Propagation is a mechanism of influence where the agent
assigns values to variables to satisfy certain conditions called
constraints. Constraint Propagation is used in many complex, real-life
scenarios of Natural Language Understanding, Computer Vision/Image
processing, etc.

249-266.Optional Reading:1. Winston Chapter 12, pages 249-266; `Click
here <http://courses.csail.mit.edu/6.034f/ai3/rest.pdf>`__
