Section 01: Preview

  *Any problem that can be solved by your in-house expert in a 10-30 minute

|Preview 1| 
Today, we’ll talk about production systems. I think you’re going to
enjoy this, because part of production systems is going to do with
learning. And this is the first time in the course we’ll be talking
about learning. Production systems are kind of cognitive architecture,
in which knowledge is represented in the form of rules. This is the last
topic under the fundamental topics part of the course. We’ll start by
talking about cognitive architectures in general, then focus on
production systems, then come to learning, a particular mechanism of
learning called chunking.
